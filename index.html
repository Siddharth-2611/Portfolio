About Me

I'm Siddharth, a Computer Science undergrad at KIIT with a focus on full-stack development and machine learning. I've built multiple projects including a task tracker, medical recommender, and a cancer detection system using modern tools like Flask, TensorFlow, and React. I love turning ideas into working products and thrive in problem-solving environments.

My journey in programming began during high school when I discovered the power of code to solve complex problems. Since then, I've been constantly learning and exploring new technologies, from building responsive web applications to developing AI-powered systems. I'm particularly drawn to full-stack development because it allows me to see projects through from conception to deployment.

When I'm not coding, you can find me contributing to open-source projects, participating in hackathons, or exploring the latest trends in technology. I believe in the importance of continuous learning and staying current with industry developments. My goal is to join a dynamic team where I can contribute meaningfully while continuing to grow as a developer and problem-solver.
